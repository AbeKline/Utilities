ABOUT THE PHOTOGRAPHER

After a twenty-year corporate career, Barry Salzman, a Harvard MBA who ran a large global media organization employing thousands of people, embarked on a career transition to pursue his passion on a full-time basis. He is a professional photographer focused primarily on lifestyle, food, and travel. Salzman is a winner of the international Golden Light award, which is judged by Jeff Rosenheim, associate photography curator of the Metropolitan Museum of Art, and Susan White, photography director for Vanity Fair. Salzman’s work has appeared in such publications as Vanity Fair, Vogue Entertaining + Travel, Harper’s Bazaar, AdNews, Australian Geographic, and OutTraveler. His work can be seen at www.barrysalzman.net.
